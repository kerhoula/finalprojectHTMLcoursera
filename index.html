Freddo Espresso Metrio

Description

A Freddo Espresso Metrio is a smooth, refreshing Greek-style iced espresso. It is made by shaking freshly brewed espresso with ice until it becomes cold and slightly foamy.

Image

Ingredients

  • 2 shots of espresso
  • 1–2 teaspoons sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes
  • A small splash of cold water (optional)

Instructions

  1. Brew 2 shots of espresso.
  2. Add sugar while the espresso is still hot and stir well.
  3. Pour the espresso into a shaker with ice.
  4. Shake for 10–15 seconds until chilled and foamy.
  5. Fill a glass with fresh ice.
  6. Strain the espresso over the ice.
  7. Add a small splash of cold water if desired, then serve immediately.
