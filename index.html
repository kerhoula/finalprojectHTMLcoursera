Freddo Espresso Metrio

A refreshing Greek-style iced espresso.

Description

A Freddo Espresso Metrio is a chilled espresso drink made by shaking hot espresso with ice until it becomes smooth and slightly foamy.

Images

Ingredients

  • 2 shots of espresso
  • 1–2 teaspoons sugar
  • Ice cubes
  • A splash of cold water (optional)

Instructions

  1. Brew 2 shots of espresso.
  2. Add sugar and stir while hot.
  3. Pour into a shaker with ice.
  4. Shake for 10–15 seconds.
  5. Strain into a glass with fresh ice.
  6. Serve immediately.
